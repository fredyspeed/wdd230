Description of the target audience

The target audience is people with purchasing power, who enjoy nature and quiet walks.



Personal

families with young children: They are the ones who can enjoy the attractions offered with purchasing power, who enjoy nature and quiet walks.

Men and women who are physically active preferably under 60 years of age due to the effort involved in mountain walks

  • People who want to rest away from a city environment

  • Those who like to stay in picturesque towns with history will visit the site.
