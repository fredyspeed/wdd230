Visual Hierarchy

Universidad veracruzana

uv.mx

On this site the images appear in order of importance, the images are links to see news and the importance is according to the date the news was published and we have the most recent news in the banner and in the images, which appear in the bottom of the page is previous news.

White Space and Clean Design

Samsung

samsung.com.mx

On this page you can see the principle of white space and clean design, there are spaces between the image and between sections of the page, this helps the user to find the information and helps to show only the content that he considers important.

Repetition

Sears

sears.com.mx

On this site you can see how the objects or images, whether they are boxes or circles, are repeated over and over again, throughout the page, identifying the objects for sale as well as their classifications, that is, very similar icons are used to represent brands or categories, as well as images in rectangles for specific products
